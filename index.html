Intro

All problems aren't so complex if you stay a second to think about them. To solve a issue you must think in calm way and leave aside the stress which is a productivity and solution solver killer.

Along my lifetime I've always appreciated the people who put effort in their work and do their very best so that they can evolve as persons alongside their skillset. You can always find benefits when a good work is done.

Since my highschool years I've always struggled with having everything I work on to be perfect. This is a double-edged sword: as a developer you need to dive into many techlogies stacks so that you can achieve it and sometimes it has its bad and goods: the time and focus spent there but the knoweldge is earned. You need to know how and when you should delegate some tasks to them, describe the work that needs to be done and help them when they have some impediments.You need to have faith in your teammates. awesome work

I wanted at first to go to a medical school university because I liked the way chemistry linked everything together and the beauty of the anatomy. As time went by and I was studying programming more in-dept i felt that this passion of mine to understand how things work easily translated to this vast it domain. I've gradually changed my mind and finally I took a big decision that I'm happy to this day, I went to Babes-Bolyai University, Informatics and Mathematics Faculty. Nothing is lost, nothing is created, everything is transformed.

Work experience

Java Software Developer

E

May 2016 - Present

Cluj-Napoca

In this period of time I was allocated on two big e-commerce projects that handle payments. The main technologies used were spring boot, spring modules, continuous delivery pipelines(jenkins, sonarqube, cloud deployment of a container like openstack/openshift, automation tests runned against the container).

By working on payments projects I gained a large knowledge technical and business wise. Being in context with multiple payment protocols best practices and various business requirements, for example the 3ds version 2, multiple payments methods implementation, recurring transactions, APMs(Alternativa payment methods).

Also, I gained experience on how products can be delivered in a continuous way as a team and how teams synchronize to deliver a bigger functionality.

In the meantime, I worked on some side-projects that were for various clients.
